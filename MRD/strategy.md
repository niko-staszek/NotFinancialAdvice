Midweek Range Division
    INSTRUMENTS: EURUSD, US500, US100
    TIMEFRAME: H1
    RISK TO REWARD: 1:2 (but maybe bigger if we find other confimation)

    1. We select High and Low from MONDAY-TUESDAY period
    2. In WEDNESDAY we make TRENDLINE 
    3a. We look to enter the trade below the trendline for bullish market
    3b. We look to enter the trade above the trendline for bearish market
    4. We favor Wednesdays when the price is outside the box so we can have clean trade
    5. if the price wanders within the box we do not open the trade
