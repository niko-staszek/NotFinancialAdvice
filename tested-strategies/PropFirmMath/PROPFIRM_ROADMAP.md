# Prop-Firm Roadmap — Dead Simple

How JJ turns a tiny trading edge into big payouts. **This is the part that makes the money** (the trade
edge alone is ~1–2%). Tables paste straight into Excel. **Not validated by us — plan, not promise.**

## The idea in one line
A prop firm = a **cheap token for a big prize**: pay ~$100 for an "evaluation," and a passed + funded
account can pay out **thousands**. Buy **many** cheap tokens; even a small edge wins more prize money than
the tokens cost. **Catch:** the firm kicks you out / shrinks your account if you win too much.

## The money math (simple)
| Step | Rough number |
|---|---|
| Chance an eval passes | ~**1 in 3** (33%) |
| Chance a funded account reaches a payout | ~**1 in 3** |
| So evals bought per payout | ~**1 in 9** |
| Cost per funded account | ~**$300** (≈ 3 evals × $100) |
| Money back per cycle | ~**3× what you spent** |
| Withdraw rule | after **5 winning days (≥$150 each)** → take out **half**, leave half, repeat |

## The phases (the roadmap)
| Phase | Money | Goal | What you do | Accounts | Biggest risk |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **0** | $0 | Learn + prove the edge | Backtest the rules; paper/sim trade till boring | 0 | Skipping this |
| **1** | $0 → $10k | Get your **first payout** | 1–2 firms; pass a few evals; bank a $1–2k payout | 1–5 | Quitting early (hardest phase) |
| **2** | $10k → $30k | Stack accounts | 10–20 funded; manage drawdown across the **whole portfolio**; reinvest ~3×/cycle | 10–20 | Over-risking one account |
| **3** | $30k → $100k/mo | Scale to the ceiling | 5–10 firms, ~5×150k each; build a slower setup for big accounts | 25–50 | **Firm bans** (the ceiling) |

## Which firms (start list)
| Tier | Firms | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Start here** | **Topstep, TradiFy, Lucid** | Pay reliably, fast, fair rules |
| Add later (for a reason) | **Apex** (hold 20 accounts), **Funded Next** (daily payouts), **E8** (no live-yank) | Add only when you've maxed the start firms |
| Avoid | Alpha (bad value), and any unknown/new firm | Bad EV or scam risk |
- **Read every firm's hidden rules before buying** (consistency %, drawdown type, payout windows, live-account traps).

## The CEILING (most important risk)
- Make ~**$50k–$100k** at one firm → they often **ban you or move you to a tiny live account that deletes the balance.**
- Defense: **rotate firms before the threshold**; keep **max ~3 accounts per firm**; spread across many firms.
- Prefer **End-of-Day trailing drawdown** (locks at 4 PM, predictable). **Avoid intraday-trailing** firms (can kill an account that spikes then dips).

## The 5 rules (tape to your monitor)
1. **One strategy only.** No switching mid-drawdown.
2. **Attack evals** — risk is capped; don't undersize and fail slowly.
3. **Many accounts, many firms** (10–15+). Spreads the luck.
4. **Daily profit target AND hard daily loss limit on every account.** Hit either → stop for the day.
5. **"$3,000 should feel like $3."** Risk units, not dollar amounts.

## Honest reality check
- This only works if the trade rules are **at least slightly profitable after costs** — which **we have not yet tested.** Step 0 (the backtest) decides if the whole thing is real.
- Don't risk money you can't lose on evals until Phase 0 passes.
